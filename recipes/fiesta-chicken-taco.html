Fiesta Slow Cooker Shredded Chicker Tacos Recipe

Picture of homemade taco attempt made by Lisa Scott.

The Recipe:

This chicken tacos recipe is easy to make in a slow cooker. Spoon the filling into warm tortillas for a very tasty meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps:

  1. Step 1 Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  2. Step 2 Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  3. Step 3 Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken.
